How To Create A Liquid Meal

  1. Gather a liquid base (water, broth, or juice) and 2–5 whole-food add-ins.
  2. Wash & Prep produce into blender-friendly pieces.
  3. Base First — pour liquid into the blender jar.
  4. Soft Then Dense — add soft fruit/veg, then denser items.
  5. Blend In Stages — low, then high until smooth.
  6. Taste, Serve, Store — drink fresh; refrigerate leftovers ≤24h.

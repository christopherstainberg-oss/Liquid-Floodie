Job Scheduling Daily reminder & auto-rotate

Run a daily “job” on this device while LiquidFloodie is open in your browser (or installed PWA). At the time you set, the app can toast a blend reminder, optionally rotate your weekly meal plan and rebuild the grocery list, and show a system notification if you allow it.

  • When it runs: once per calendar day, when the clock matches Hour + Minute (checked about every 30 seconds while the app is open).
  • Reminder: shows an in-app toast; with Web notifications enabled (and permission granted), also shows a browser/OS notification.
  • Auto-rotate: when the job fires, rotates all weekly meals and refreshes the grocery list (same idea as “Rotate All Meals”).
  • Device-only: jobs do not run in the cloud—keep this tab or PWA open (or reopen it near the scheduled time) so the timer can fire.
